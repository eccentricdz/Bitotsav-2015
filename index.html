      • nights

      • fusion night

        19th march 2015

        First of the five magnificent nights, this night showcases a drama performed by our very own Dramsoc! ‘Kavi Samelan’ is going to be the accent of the night, which in history has witnessed megashows by Kumar Vishwas in Bitotsav’11, Rivaldo (aka Nitin Gupta) in Bitotsav’12 and Surendra Sharma in Bitotsav ’14. The lively and enlightening display from the literary world will be followed by soothing fusion music and will bring in limelight SPIC MACAY (The Society for the Promotion of Indian Classical Music And Culture Amongst Youth). This is one night with a blend of ethnicity and resounding grooves pledged to leave you entranced.

      • star night

        20th march 2015

        FMagnificent Bollywood music ringing through the air, we bring to you a night to sing along with an eternally loved popular voice live before you! So get ready to fist-pump the air and come out and enjoy your heart out in the Star Night. With groups like Rohan&Dance group, Fictitious Group and D-Maniax, Bitotsav has been getting the best of contemporary performances every year. This would be the night to look out for!

      • band night

        22nd march 2015

        Music washes away the dust from the soul of everyday life and when it comes to western music, there is nothing more to be told. BITOTSAV’15 awaits to enrapture you with the best of western rock cusped by your idols. Pull up your gears, put up your favourite tees because this time it is going to be invigorating. With so many bands battling for the top spot, be sure of witnessing the best of them and shaking to your favourite tunes. Without music life would be a mistake and the BAND NIGHT has always been a testimony of BITOTSAV’s ability to rock the world.

      • techno music night

        21st march 2015

        Long gone are the days when people would gather round and shake a leg to folk music or sway to slow indie pop. It's the 21st century and Techno Music is all the rage. There are no routines. There are no judges. All you have to do is dance like no one's watching. Let loose and allow the music to take over you, as your body moves to the exhilarating tunes and your heart thumps to the pounding bass. From techno to house and dubstep to trance, the stage is set for an electrifying night of some seriously awesome partying!

      • pro night

        23rd march 2015

        Was your day bad? Was your day good? However it might have been, I bet you this very night would clamour for all the pep you possess. Here is the most awaited night of BITOTSAV’15 with a pro on the limelight keeping you deadly alive to every verse, every tune and every beet. With the night being the end to all the fun we would have, assures you an exemplary experience with an everlasting recapture in your lives. So gear up folks to match up to the spirits and make up to the PRO NIGHT.

        empowerment

        Women are the pivot of our society. It's very unfortunate that in a country like ours, women are worshiped like deities one hand, and at the same time, literacy rate stands at mere 55% and cases of Gender Discrimination go up day by day. This BITOTSAV, we stand for Women Empowerment, understanding the fact that no nation can develop without a strong womenfolk. events like mass painting, guest lecture series will mark our support for WOMEN EMPOWERMENT,a cause to benefit all
