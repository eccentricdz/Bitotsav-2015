      • dhrupad brothers, MAD

        heritage night

        19th march 2015

        Perceive pacifying taste of Indian classical music by Drupad Brothers. From the soothed harmonious ambience to light comical environment in "Hasya Kavi Sammelan". The night gets mightier as MAD (Mad About Drama) presents a theatrical play on "Codename SRK".

      • dj lost stories and dj sartek

        EDM night

        20th march 2015

        Jive to the beats of Electronic Dance Music with two of the most distinguished DJs featuring DJ Lost Stories and DJ Sartek.

      • F-16, white light

        band night

        21st march 2015

        Be a part of the finale of the night witnessing western category of the Battle of Bands "White Light". Also we have in store a glorious performance by "The F‐16" band.

      • parikrama, lagori, aalankrit

        fusion night

        22nd march 2015

        Ensure your presence on the finale of the eastern category of Battle of Bands "Aalankrit" clubbed with high quotient performance by the band "Lagori" and "Parikrama".

      • salim sulaiman, shweta pandit, vipul mehra

        pro night

        23rd march 2015

        The star night of Bitotsav'15 draped in ardour and melody with India's one of the leading musical brothers "Salim‐Sulaiman". To add to the night's enchantment we have Shweta Pandit and Vipul Mehra who will escort you to the heaven of music through the night.

      • women

        empowerment

        Women are the pivot of our society. It's very unfortunate that in a country like ours, women are worshiped like deities one hand, and at the same time, literacy rate stands at mere 55% and cases of Gender Discrimination go up day by day. This BITOTSAV, we stand for Women Empowerment, understanding the fact that no nation can develop without a strong womenfolk. events like mass painting, guest lecture series will mark our support for WOMEN EMPOWERMENT,a cause to benefit all
