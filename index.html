        E-boot Camp provides the platform for young and budding entrepreneurs to improvise and fund their start-up. An opportunity for entrepreneurs from across India to compete and win Prize money worth Rs. 50,000!!

        Round 1

        Online B-plan submission. Participants to submit their B-plan (executive summary) based on the given format and submit them to ebootcamp@bitotsav.in latest by 20th February, 2015. All entries must have, "Round1_[Team Name]" as subject to the mail.

        RULES

        1. A team can contain maximum 5 members.
        2. All teams participating in E-boot Camp must register themselves at the given link:- bit.ly/ebootcamp15
        3. If a team is selected for the final round, the team should bear all its expenses and accommodation fees as per BITOTSAV '15 norms.
        4. Plagiarism is strictly prohibited and sources have to be clearly stated.
        5. Every contestant must agree with the rules of the contest and must accept the declaration of confidentiality during registration in order to participate. Please make sure you are aware of the Terms and Conditions of the competition before registering.

        Prize Money

        1. Winner - Rs. 25,000 plus ebay vouchers
        2. 1st Runner Up - Rs. 15,000 plus ebay vouchers
        3. 2nd Runner Up - Rs. 10,000 plus ebay vouchers
